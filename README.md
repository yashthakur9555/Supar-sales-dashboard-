# Supar-sales-dashboard-

🎯 Objective of the Dashboard 

To analyze superstore sales performance across regions, segments, shipping modes, and time, while tracking revenue, profit, and operational efficiency (delivery time) to support strategic business decisions.

📊 Business Questions Answered by This Dashboard

💰 Sales & Profit Performance

What is the total sales and total profit generated?
Is the business growing year-over-year (2019 vs 2020)?
Which months show peak and low sales performance?
Are there months where profit does not align with sales?

📈 Trend & Growth Analysis

How does monthly sales trendchange across years?
Is there a consistent growth pattern or seasonality?
Which months contribute most to annual revenue growth?

🌍 Regional Performance

Which region (West, East, Central, South) generates the highest sales?
Which region is underperforming and needs attention?
How should the company allocate resources geographically?

👥 Customer Segment Analysis

Which customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) contributes most to revenue?
Should the business focus more on a specific segment?
Are there opportunities to grow smaller segments?

🚚 Shipping & Operations

Which shipping mode is most used (Standard, Second, First Class, Same Day)?
Does higher usage of certain shipping modes impact cost or profit?
What is the average delivery time, and is it efficient?

🛒 Product Performance

Which sub-categories (Phones, Chairs, Binders, etc.) generate the most sales?
Are there low-performing product categories that should be improved or removed?
Which products should be prioritized for inventory and marketing?

💳 Payment Behavior

What are the most preferred payment methods (COD, Online, Cards)?
Is there a high dependency on COD, and does it pose a risk?
How can the business encourage digital payments?
